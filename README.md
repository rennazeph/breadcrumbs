3.2.Feedback and Review Process
Benefits of Early and Frequent Stakeholder Feedback.

Little or deficient stakeholder involvement during the SDLC may result in the product diverging from the stakeholder's original or current vision. This can cost from reworks, missed deadlines or even project failure.

A frequent stakeholder feedback in the cycle helps preventing any misunderstanding that may change requirements or previous implementations.
A good communication improves the understanding of what everyone's building.
But most importantly.
Show value to stakeholders !
(And identifies risks)

Stakeholders, naturally, vary on how they would present this feedback, and so the  ISO/IEC 20246 standard provides a basic framework of what activies constitute a good review. This may become more specific on determined areas at specific cycle times and needs.
